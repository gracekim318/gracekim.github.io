Description
Thom Browne is a NYC-based professional, modern, luxury brand and this is a rebranded website of Thom Browne that portrays the brand's personality - individuality within uniformity - through their redefinement and reinterpretation of both women and men's formal wear. Thom Browne consistently challenges themselves to create something new. Proportion and precise tailoring are the unique elements of Thom Browne and officially an essential part of the fashion dictionary as they were once considered provocative in the past.
